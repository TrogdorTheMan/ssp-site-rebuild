Now

The Pirates have staged a dramatic landing on Alki Beach every summer since the early years of Seafair — the tradition is unchanged, the crowd as eager as ever.

Neptune's Court

Now

King Neptune and Queen Alcyone have presided over the waters of Puget Sound since Seafair's founding — the costumes have evolved, the ceremony endures.
