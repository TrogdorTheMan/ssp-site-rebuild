Meet the crew — 40+ scallywags keeping Seafair tradition alive since 1949

The Seattle Seafair Pirates are a rotating crew of community volunteers, performers, and civic champions. Each brings their own brand of roguishness to the deck — but all share a commitment to charitable service, Seafair tradition, and a healthy disrespect for Neptune's authority.
